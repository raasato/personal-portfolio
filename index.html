About Me

Aloha! My name is Robynne. I am first a mother of two but also a student with Arizona State University Online Campus taking courses from the beautiful island of Oahu, Hawai'i. I have earned a Bachelor's degree in Food and Nutrition Entrepreneurship in May 2026. During my time with ASU, I've taken additional courses to learn HTML, CSS, and JavaScript and was recently accepted to the Graphic Information Technology Graduate program with a focus on web development.

This portfolio showcases the projects I'm building as I learn.

Hobbies & Interests
